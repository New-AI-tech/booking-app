Effective Date: April 30, 2026

Responsible Entity: Ahmed Backer (Egypt)

1. What data do we collect?

When a user connects their Instagram Business account, we receive the following data from Meta: Instagram User ID, message text content, timestamps, and basic public profile information of the connected Instagram Business account.

2. Why do we collect and process this data?

The sole purpose of collecting and processing this data is to provide an AI-powered automatic reply service for Instagram Direct Messages.

3. Who do we share your data with?

We use third-party service providers (data processors) to operate our service:

  • Render (Render Services, Inc.): Hosts our bot’s server.
  • OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.): Receives the incoming message text to generate an appropriate reply.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. How long do we keep your data?

We retain conversation logs for a maximum of 30 days. After that period, they are automatically deleted from our server.

5. Your rights (access, deletion, correction)

Users have the right to ask what data we hold, request deletion of their data, or correct inaccurate data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at theyellowstone796@gmail.com.

6. Data security

We take data protection seriously. We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all API calls to ensure your data is transmitted securely.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at theyellowstone796@gmail.com.
