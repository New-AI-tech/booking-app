Data Deletion Instructions for inschatbot

If you no longer wish to use inschatbot and want your data removed, you can easily request a full data deletion. When a deletion is processed, we will permanently remove all message texts, logs containing your Instagram User ID, and any temporary cached data associated with your account from our servers.

How to Request Data Deletion

Please follow this step-by-step guide to request the deletion of your data:

Step 1: Send an email to theyellowstone796@gmail.com.
Step 2: In the email subject line, write exactly: "Instagram Data Deletion Request".
Step 3: In the email body, include your Instagram username (or Instagram User ID) and clearly state your request for data deletion.
Step 4: We will process the deletion of your data within 72 hours of receiving your request. Once the deletion is complete, we will send you a confirmation email.

Automatic Deletion Policy

Please note that even if you do not manually request deletion, inschatbot automatically deletes all conversation logs and associated message data 30 days after the last message was processed.

Contact
